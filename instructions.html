~How to Use~

Demo Mode

  1. set the bingo card size (4x4 or 5x5) and seed value. Seed values can be entered directly or randomly generated using the “Generate Seed” button.
  2. click the “Generate Sheet” button with the seed values entered to generate a bingo sheet.
    • Left Click: Fill red color
    • Right Click: Fill green color
  3. Press the “take a screenshot” button at the bottom of the generated bingo sheet to save the bingo sheet and seed information as an image.
  4. click on the “Goal List” link and the lottery list will appear in a separate tab.

CSV Mode

  1. Prepare a CSV file.
    *Enter the square information one by one in column A. By creating one blank line, the listings above the blank line will be drawn outside the center square, and the listings below the blank line will be drawn in the center square. *If the list is less than 25 songs, the bingo sheet cannot be created.
  2. click the “Select File” button in the “Import CSV:” area and select a locally stored CSV file. the CSV file must be in a format where the song names are listed in column A and the list is divided by a blank line.
  3. If the file is successfully imported, “(Import Completed)” will appear on the right side of the file name.
  4. click the “Generate Sheet” button with the seed values entered to generate a bingo sheet.
    • Left Click: Fill red color
    • Right Click: Fill green color
  5. Press the “take a screenshot” button at the bottom of the generated bingo sheet to save the bingo sheet and seed information as an image.
  6. click on the “Goal List” link and the lottery list will appear in a separate tab.

Google Sheet Mode

  1. Prepare a Google Sheet.
    *Enter the square information one by one in column A. By creating one blank line, the listings above the blank line will be drawn outside the center square, and the listings below the blank line will be drawn in the center square. *If the list is less than 25 songs, the bingo sheet cannot be created. *The created Google Sheet should be made public.
  2. Paste the URL of the published Google Spreadsheet into the “Enter Google Sheet URL:” input field and press the “Load” button.
  3. If the data is successfully imported, “Load Completed” is displayed on the right side of the input field and the data is saved.
  4. click the “Generate Sheet” button with the seed values entered to generate a bingo sheet.
    • Left Click: Fill red color
    • Right Click: Fill green color
  5. Press the “take a screenshot” button at the bottom of the generated bingo sheet to save the bingo sheet and seed information as an image.
  6. click on the “Goal List” link and the lottery list will appear in a separate tab.
